About Us

Building Trusted Healthcare in Bihar.

Director, MR Hospital Healthcare Leader Institution Builder Patna, Bihar

Mohammad Saad is building trusted healthcare in Bihar through MR Hospital in Patna. The focus is on compassionate care, strong systems and long-term public impact.

With a people-first approach, the vision is to make dependable treatment more accessible while building an institution families can trust with confidence.

The journey is not only about growth, but about creating a healthcare environment where families feel heard, respected and supported at every stage of care.

Through disciplined leadership and community-rooted thinking, the aim is to build lasting credibility for both Mohammad Saad and MR Hospital across Bihar.
